Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a land?

Browse available lands, click on a listing you're interested in, and click the "Book Now" button. Fill in your details and submit your booking request. The owner will review and approve it.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash payments. Payment details will be provided after your booking is approved by the land owner.

How do I list my land?

Create an account, upgrade to a Land Owner account, then click "List Land" from your dashboard. Fill in all the required details about your property and submit for review.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel pending bookings at any time. For approved bookings, please contact the land owner directly or reach out to our support team for assistance.

How do I contact a land owner?

Once logged in, you can send messages to land owners through our messaging system. Contact details may also be provided after booking approval.
