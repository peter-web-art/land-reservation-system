How the System Works

Every protected action starts with account creation. After that, each role gets the right interface and permissions.

01

Create an Account

Start from the landing page, create your account, then choose whether you are an owner or a customer.

02

Owners Submit Verified Land

Owners upload land details, government verification, images, and request approvals for admin review.

03

Customers Book Farmland

Customers browse approved posts, request bookings, and track pending and approved farmland handover periods.

Role-Based Access

The system changes after sign-in so each user sees only the tools relevant to their responsibilities.

🧑‍🌾

For Land Owners

  • Add land with specification and images.
  • Submit listings for admin approval and verification.
  • Approve or decline incoming bookings.
🧑‍🤝‍🧑

For Customers

  • Browse approved farmland posts across Tanzania.
  • Make booking requests with your account.
  • Track pending and approved handover periods.
🛡️

For Administrators

  • Review land submissions and verification status.
  • Approve or reject listings before they go live.
  • Monitor bookings and platform activity.
